History of Cricket

Cricket originated in England in the 16th century and has become one of the most-loved sports globally, especially in India, England, Australia, and South Africa.

Formats of the Game

Cricket is played in three major formats: Test (5-day), ODI (50 overs), and T20 (20 overs).

Why Cricket is Special

Cricket combines skill, strategy, teamwork, and passion — and unites countries through tournaments like the ICC World Cup and IPL.
